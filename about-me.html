About Me

Hi, my name is Habdul Hazeez, I have a formal education in Computer Science and I teach and write code with interests in Web development, Computer Security, and Artificial Intelligence.

Currently, Web development takes most of my time and I use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL database to develop Web applications.

Aside Web development, Computer Security and Artificial Intelligence are two fields that really interests me. I read everything I can find on them including blog posts, articles and research paper and I publish Security news weekly round-up on DEV.

I write articles on what I have learned. You will find my articles on DEV under the username ziizium.

In addition, I can read code in Python, C and C++.

I am constantly learning and I'll learn a technology from scratch if it's the fit for the project at hand including but not limited to programming languages, libraries, frameworks and tools.

You can find me on Twitter, Instagram, DEV and Dribbble. If email is your thing you can mail me at ziizium [@] protonmail [.] ch.
